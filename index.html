What major problems a Beginner could

face while dealing with stock market?

Lack of Conceptual Understanding
Beginners start trading without understanding foundational concepts of stock market and basic technical know-how. Learning is an ongoing process
Losses from Uniformed Trading
Most beginners do random buy & sell trades. They do not really know what is the trade-set up and investment rationale behind these trades
Untrustworthy & Unintelligent Tips
Tips from friends, family, office colleagues, rumors, news, fraud tip providers etc are not followed by intelligent research and analysis process to generate ideas
Inconsistent P&L
They have an intermittent streak of profit and losses in their stock market dealings. Getting consistent profits and returns is one of their biggest problems
Lack of Solid Strategies
Many intermediate participants cannot generate their own ideas and strategies and end up trading and investing using unrefined and sub-optimal strategies
Understanding different financial instruments
